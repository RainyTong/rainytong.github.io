About me


I am a CS Ph.D. student at the University of California, Davis, advised by Professor Cindy Rubio-González.

Prior to UC Davis, I got my B.E. degree in Computer Science at Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Shenzhen, China.

My research interests include Software Engineering, Deep Learning, High Performance Computing, and Large Language Models.
